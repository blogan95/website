Background

I was born and raised in Cleveland, Ohio. I was accepted to go to high school at Cleveland School of the Arts, first as a theatre major and then as a creative writing major. When I graduated in 2013, I started my next fall semester as a freshman at Kent State University, where I majored in Teaching English as a Second Language (TESL) and minored in Japanese and in Education. The summer after my fourth year, I was able to study abroad at Nihon University in Tokyo, Japan. Currently, I am participating in the Cleveland Codes program with Cuyahoga Community College to become a software developer.

Goals

I have always considered myself to be ambitious. Since I was twelve years old, my dream had been to visit Japan. Now that I have acheived that, I know that anything is possible.

In the next five years, I would like to get more involved in the community and help the younger generation in my city. I hope to go back to college and finish my bachelor's and get a job as a developer. Now that I am learning more about code, I hope to put more work into my personal project, which will be a branching narrative, visual novel style game that I have been tinkering with for awhile.
